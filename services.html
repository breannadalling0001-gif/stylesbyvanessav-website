Services

Vanessa offers a variety of protective styles and natural hair services. Final pricing depends on hair length, thickness, style complexity, and size.

Braids

  • Knotless braids (small, medium, large, any length)
  • Box braids (classic and trendy variations)
  • Feed-in / stitch braids
  • Lemonade or side braids
  • Kids’ braids (gentle and age-appropriate styles)

Locs & Natural

  • Starter locs (for clients beginning their loc journey)
  • Loc retwist and maintenance (all loc types)
  • Two-strand twists for natural hair
  • Natural hair cornrows, flat twists, or protective parting
  • Consultations on hair health and growth strategies

Crochet & More

  • Crochet braids (bohemian, curly, wavy)
  • Crochet faux locs / passion twists
  • Protective crochet styles for everyday wear or events
  • Extensions installation guidance and care tips
