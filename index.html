About Vanessa

Vanessa is a professional braider and natural hair stylist serving clients in both Connecticut and New York. She specializes in clean parts, long-lasting protective styles, knotless braids, loc maintenance, and crochet installs. Every appointment is done with care for your natural hair, edges, and overall comfort.

Serving Areas

Mobile and in-studio appointments in select parts of CT & NYC.

Connecticut: Bridgeport (main), Stratford, Fairfield, Trumbull, Norwalk, Stamford and nearby cities.

New York: Queens, Valley Stream, Jamaica, Laurelton, Rosedale and surrounding areas.

Booking & Policies

Booking is done through Instagram DMs. Please read before requesting an appointment.

  • How to book: DM @stylesbyvanessav with your name, desired style, preferred date/time, and a recent photo of your hair.
  • Deposits: A small non-refundable deposit may be required to secure your spot.
  • Preparation: Arrive with clean, detangled hair and no heavy oils or grease.
  • Lateness: Short grace period, then a late fee or cancellation may apply.
  • Kids: Child must be able to sit for the full style and be accompanied by an adult.
