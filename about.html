About Vanessa

Vanessa is a highly skilled professional braider and natural hair stylist with years of experience transforming and maintaining beautiful, healthy hair. She serves clients in Connecticut and New York, providing personalized styles that enhance your natural beauty. Vanessa specializes in knotless braids, box braids, starter locs, crochet styles, and other protective hairstyles. Every client is treated with care, from the cleanliness of parts to the longevity of each style.

Vanessa believes in creating not just a style, but a full hair experience. Her focus is on hair health, attention to detail, and a professional, welcoming environment. From children to adults, every client leaves confident and satisfied with a look that is tailored to their lifestyle and preferences.

Passionate about natural hair, Vanessa stays up-to-date with the latest techniques, trends, and products to ensure your hair looks its best and stays healthy. Your appointment isn’t just a service—it’s an experience designed to make you feel beautiful, comfortable, and confident.
